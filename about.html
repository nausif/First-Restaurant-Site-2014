ABOUT US


Since the opening in 2007, our goal and mission is to ensure customer’s satisfaction by using only the freshest and finest ingredients in our food preparation and providing an exceptional and friendly dining experience for our guests. Thai Lotus Restaurant with its dedicated team, has been serving Pakistan cuisine in Tequesta for more than 20 years. Our wide selection of delicious food ranging from traditional Thai styles of cooking - Pad Thai to modern-creative of Japanese sushi – O.M.G Roll will satisfy any insatiate appetite.
