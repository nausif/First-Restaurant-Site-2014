FAQ's

What is Nausif's Restaurant?

Nausif's Restaurant is an online food ordering system. Give your area and chose you favorite restaurant from a list of restaurants providing delivery in your area, place your order in just one click, and get the food delivered at your door step. Nausif's Restaurant will not charge you anything; payment will be on delivery or collection.

Why become a member of Nausif's Restaurant

By registering with FoodClub.pk, You receive exclusive Discount offers and promotions free of cost which are not available anywhere, weekly updates of new restaurants and discount deals by email every week, You can re-order your saved meals in just one clicks, store your delivery address, build a list of your favorite restaurants, leave reviews and comments on the restaurants you have ordered from, and also many chances to win free meals and expensive gifts. You can also pre-order your meal hours or a day in advance, giving you a chance to plan your routine ahead of time.

Do you charge customers membership fees?

No, that is the great thing about our service. There are no membership fees or any hidden charges, our services and membership cards are completely free!

How do I become a member?

Click the signup tab on the home page and Sign-up for free Once you have registered, you will be able to order yummylicious food from hundreds of restaurants in your area!
